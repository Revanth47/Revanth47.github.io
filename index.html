ABOUT ME

I am a junior year undergraduate pursuing Mechanical Engineering at National Institue of Technology,Trichy(NITT)

My areas of interest revolves around programming. Being a part of the university's coding club Delta Force I've gained a lot of exposure across various fields of programming, especially Web Development. I love to hack new stuff thats interesting and/or useful. Being in Delta incited a drive in me towards programming which I've never experienced before.

I aspire to work in a code frenzy environment where everyone is not only passionate but also crazy about what they do. I hope to be a integral part of the team and apply anything and everything I've learnt so far to ensure the work is completed in time and to everyone's satisfaction.

Pursuing Mechanical Engineering has never been a barrier towards programming, but has been the catalyst to drive my passion even faster. Learning new stuff and hacking something cool with it has become a hobby for a lifetime(hopefully).
